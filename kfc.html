KFC

KFC, or Kentucky Fried Chicken, is a global fast-food chain known for its signature fried chicken. Founded in 1930, it has become one of the world's largest and most recognizable fast-food brands, with a focus on its secret blend of 11 herbs and spices. KFC operates in numerous countries, offering a menu that typically includes fried chicken, chicken sandwiches, and various side dishes.
